GAME

Only you can help deliver the Zoombinis from the clutches of the evil Bloats. who have seized control of Zoombini Isle! Guiding the Zoombinis to a new homeland will require your best creative and logical thinking, as the way ahead is perilous. Packed with puzzles,The Logical Adventure of the Zoombinis takes players - and the Zoombinis - on an incredible journey from Zoombini Isle to a new utopia, with challenges galore along the way.

 

Players will learn and hone math and logic skills, while solving mind-bending puzzles. The Zoombinis teach and reinforce valuable life skills, including deductive reasoning, pattern recognition, hypothesis testing, and more.

 

With your help, we'll deliver the Zoombinis to their new home on iOS and Android devices in 2015.

LEARNING OPPORTUNITIES

The players’ goal is to help the Zoombinis reach their new home. They achieve this by using logic and reasoning to solve unique problems they find along their adventure. Every puzzle they encounter is different which makes for a challenging and fun experience every time. Players have to think on their feet and learn how to find solutions in any situation.

 

Players learn important life skills such as algebraic thinking, data analysis, theory formulation, and more in a fun and engaging setting. With four levels of increasing difficulty, players are constantly challenged, improving their problem-solving skills as they advance through the game.
